Process to solve a problem

i-      Analyze your problem -> input + output, constraints(data types)
ii-     Break down problem into smaller subparts
iii-    Remember/enlist the concepts
iv-     Take 2 or 3 examples before proceeding to clear out the confusion gap (don't assume)
v-      Write a Pseudo code on a paper
vi-     Dry run it once
vii-    Write down your solution.
viii-   Look out for the edge cases or boundary condition
